How We Calculate This

This calculator is based on the "Productivity Value Gap" framework from the whitepaper "The Need to Turnaround Turnover" by Mirko Kovacevic, VP of Customer Success at InForce.

1. Idle Time

2 weeks to find replacement while paying full rate with zero productivity

2. Onboarding Lag

5 days waiting for system access and permissions while paying full rate

3. Acclimation Period

30-60 days ramping to full productivity while paying full rate

All assumptions are based on industry research and validated against InForce's whitepaper data. We use conservative estimates to ensure credibility.
